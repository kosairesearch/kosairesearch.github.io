In October 2020, the company established a wholly owned subsidiary, PN Lab, to strengthen its online and home-appliance competitiveness. Distribution reaches approximately 492 trading partners spanning offline channels—department stores, hypermarkets, home-shopping networks, and electronics retailers—as well as its own online store, open-market platforms, special procurement, and a rental service. Export markets include Japan, the Americas (U.S. and Canada), Germany, the UK, and the Middle East, with new buyer development pursued through KOTRA, KITA, and major international trade fairs such as Frankfurt Ambiente and the China Canton Fair.
