Business

AutoCrypt was spun off from PentaSecurity in 2019 and listed on KOSDAQ via the technology special-listing track in 2025. Its business model rests on three pillars: (1) security consulting and CSMS/VTA certification services for OEMs and Tier suppliers (approximately 66.2% of 2025 revenue); (2) supply of in-vehicle security (IVS), V2X communication security, PKI, and post-quantum cryptography (PQC) solutions across six product lines; and (3) royalty revenue tied to the volume of mass-produced ECUs equipped with its security software.
