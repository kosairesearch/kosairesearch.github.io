Business

Founded in 2013 and listed on KOSDAQ in 2020 via a SPAC merger, Wisebirds is a digital ad-tech and agency company affiliated with the Daou-Kiwoom Group. It is the only Korean agency holding top-tier partnerships with all 11 major platforms—Meta, Google, TikTok, Kakao, and Naver among others—enabling a one-stop solution for both inbound Korean campaigns by foreign advertisers and outbound global campaigns by domestic brands. In early 2024, the company acquired Adefficiency, a search advertising (SA) specialist, for ₩74bn at 100% ownership, establishing an integrated DA+SA operating structure with a combined workforce exceeding 400 employees. The group's 2025 advertising billings reached ₩579.2bn, up 24.6% YoY, the highest growth rate among Korea's top 10 advertising companies per the Korea Advertiser Federation. Its proprietary AI ad-automation platform, Nest Ads Manager (NAM), provides publishers with an SDK/API-based SaaS advertising server so they can operate their own ad business, adding a revenue-sharing model on top of traditional agency commissions. The platform counts 14 clients—including Daum, Saramin, Blind, Hanatour, LotteON, Danawa, and Enuri.com—with no direct domestic competitors, creating structural customer lock-in. Following an exclusive co-advertising partnership with Ably and Korea's first TikTok Marketing Partners designation in 2025, the company launched a dedicated US pharmaceutical DTC advertising team in June 2026 to support Korean biotech and healthcare companies seeking US market entry. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) services and retail media are also expanding, with Adefficiency and Wisebirds Japan as consolidated subsidiaries.
