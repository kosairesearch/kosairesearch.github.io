Business

Korea BNC is a biomaterial-based medical device and biotech company established in 2007 and listed on KOSDAQ in 2019, whose core business is developing and manufacturing aesthetic medical devices using hyaluronic acid (HA) and collagen as raw materials, including dermal fillers and botulinum toxin. The Sejong GMP facility is designated as the core production infrastructure to support expansion from medical devices into biopharmaceuticals.
