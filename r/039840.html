Business

Domestically, DIO competes against Osstem Implant (market leader), Dentium, and MegaGen; internationally, it faces global majors like Straumann and Dentsply Sirona as well as rapidly expanding Chinese domestic brands. A three-tier product lineup (premium, value, economy) enables coverage from high-value advanced markets to price-sensitive emerging markets.
