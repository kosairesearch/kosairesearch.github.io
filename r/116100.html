The company maintains IATF 16949 and ISO 14001 dual certifications and manages outgoing defect rates below 1 ppm across all product lines. On the diversification front, entry into the medical device and healthcare segment—air quality monitors and walking aids—provides a partial hedge against automotive cycle volatility.
