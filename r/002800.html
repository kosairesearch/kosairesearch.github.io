The Sejong facility is the company's manufacturing and R&D hub, with progressive automation investments having improved cost competitiveness, with the adhesive patch production line utilization exceeding 70%. In 2025, Sinsin secured an exclusive OTC distribution agreement with Buhwang Pharma for six brands across nine products through 2028, leveraging its pharmacy network for product diversification. On the R&D front, the company is developing microneedle pharmaceuticals using its proprietary TDDS technology, having completed both coated and dissolvable microneedle research facilities and filed PCT international patents.
