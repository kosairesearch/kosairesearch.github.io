Its front-end industries are diverse, spanning displays, automotive electronics, and industrial equipment, with Samsung Display, LG Display, and BOE among its key customers. The environmental solutions segment offers comprehensive air management products, including ionizers, dust sensors, and collection filters, for use in air purifiers, air conditioners, and cleanrooms. The company is expanding smart home and industrial environment solutions by leveraging synergies with the IoT sensor technology of its affiliate Crepas. As a compact specialist manufacturer with approximately 125 employees, its competitive edge lies in deepening core technology and maintaining close customer relationships rather than broad diversification.
