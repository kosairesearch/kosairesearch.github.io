Business

Founded in 2015 and listed on KOSDAQ in 2021, Wanted Lab is an HR tech company operating 'Wanted,' an AI-driven recruitment matching platform as its flagship service. The platform connects over 3.6 million job seekers with approximately 35,000 corporate clients; its AI algorithm, trained on more than 10 million matching records, reportedly boosts document-screen pass rates more than fourfold versus unguided applications. As of Q1 2026, revenue is split approximately 70% from recruitment AI, 23% from AX new business, and 7% from other services, reflecting a structural shift away from success-fee-based matching toward subscription and packaged AX solutions. The AX segment evolved from 'Wanted LaaS (LLM as a Service)' and was officially rebranded in April 2026 as 'Ennoia,' an enterprise AI agent generation and operations solution that bundles employee AI training, in-house AI environment setup, and AI-assisted talent management. The platform is being sold via both on-premises and SaaS channels. Supplementary services include the freelancer matching platform 'Wanted Gigs,' corporate information platform 'Wanted Insight' (formerly Creditjob), and the beta 'Wanted Global' foreign-talent recruitment service. Key competitors include Worksfear (formerly Jobkorea, which acquired Jobplanet), SaraminHR, and Incruit—all accelerating AI integration and intensifying competition. Headcount stands at roughly 167 as of April 2026, with headquarters at Lotte World Tower in Songpa, Seoul.
