Business

AztecWB (formerly Wangbeol Mulsan, established 1969, rebranded 2020) is a Busan-based woolen fabric specialist listed on KOSDAQ since 2000. Its operations are divided into a Fabric Division — comprising a Fashion Market Materials segment serving menswear and womenswear brands, and a Uniform Materials segment for corporate and institutional clients — and a Clothing Division operating the 'AVAMOLLI' and 'Rossum' brands under a fashion promotion unit. The Busan factory in Saha-gu offers end-to-end vertical integration from raw materials through spinning, dyeing, weaving, and finishing, extending to knitwear design and garment production as a one-stop system. The company has built an AI-driven big-data clothing promotion platform to serve ODM demand from luxury and online brands. It is also developing functional woolen fabrics and entering the workwear materials market, while launching a golf-promotion division to reach the sportswear segment. The competitive backdrop is one of structural domestic market contraction, with pressure from global SPA brands and imported fabrics. The company's vertically integrated quality capabilities and top-tier domestic market position in woolen fabrics remain its core differentiating factors.
