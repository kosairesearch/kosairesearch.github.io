The product lineup covers the full interior spectrum—from basic injection-molded trim pieces to premium fabric-laminated components including pillar trims, package trays, inside handles, armrests, and luggage screens. As the flagship affiliate of Seoyon Group, the company coordinates closely with group companies Seoyon Intech, Seoyon Auto Vision, and Seoyon Top Metal.
