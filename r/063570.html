The business is organized into three pillars: Cash (ATM VAN, integrated ATM management, and cash transport), Mobility (unmanned parking and EV charging), and Unmanned Solutions (kiosk and video security). A dedicated Mobility Division integrates the parking and EV charging businesses to capture cross-operational synergies.
