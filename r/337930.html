Its flagship Xexymix brand targets yoga, pilates, running, and gym-wear markets; the company expanded its portfolio in April 2024 by launching seamless inner-wear sub-brand Mellowday. The global athleisure market grew from approximately $233B in 2019 to $397B in 2024, and Korean brands' 'Asian fit' design advantage and K-content brand synergies provide favorable conditions for regional expansion.
