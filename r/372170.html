Business

Yunsung F&C is a specialty machinery manufacturer founded in 1986 with mixing technology as its core competency, listed on KOSDAQ in November 2022. Its primary business is the supply of raw material mixing equipment and systems for lithium-ion battery electrode manufacturing, specifically the slurry preparation stage. The company co-developed the world's first and largest-capacity PD Mixer and successfully mass-produced a Main Mixer with Samsung SDI, and was also the first globally to implement an IoT-integrated slurry mixing system. Its differentiated EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction) turn-key delivery model—covering design, engineering, procurement, fabrication, installation, and automation—creates high switching costs for customers. Beyond batteries, the company produces mixing equipment for pharmaceutical, bio, and food sectors, including a bio-factory project for Daewoong Bio's CDMO operations worth approximately KRW 13 billion. Yunsung also participates in government-funded R&D programs for next-generation continuous slurry manufacturing and dry-process mixing systems. With a high export ratio, it recorded USD 130–150 million in annual exports in 2021–2022 and received the '100 Million USD Export Tower' award in 2023. Revenue is highly concentrated among a small number of major global battery cell manufacturers, making earnings strongly correlated with customers' capex cycles.
