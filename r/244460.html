Business

OliPass was founded in 2006 and listed on KOSDAQ in 2019 via the technology-exception route. The core technology is its proprietary OPNA (OliPass PNA) platform, which uses cell-permeable Peptide Nucleic Acid molecules to bind Pre-mRNA within the cell nucleus, inducing exon skipping to suppress disease-causing protein production or restore normal protein function. Key pipelines include OLP-1002 (a non-opioid analgesic targeting Nav1.7 channels), STMN2-OPNA (an ALS candidate targeting STMN2 gene expression normalization), and OLP-1003 (for age-related macular degeneration).
