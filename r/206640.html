Business

Boditech Med, founded in 1998 and listed on KOSDAQ in 2014, is a specialist in Point-of-Care Testing (POCT) within the in-vitro diagnostics (IVD) sector, operating two business divisions: immunoassay cartridges and diagnostic instruments. The product portfolio spans 120+ disease categories — infectious diseases, diabetes, cardiovascular conditions, cancer, and hormones — built on lateral flow immunofluorescence technology. The flagship automated AFIAS and versatile ichroma platforms each support over 100 compatible diagnostic cartridges, enabling a razor-and-blades business model where device placements drive recurring consumable revenue; the COVID-era surge in global device installations significantly expanded the recurring revenue base. The company distributes across 130+ countries, with MENA representing approximately 25% of revenue and Asia approximately 19%. Production spans Chuncheon HQ (Korea), U.S. and Chinese subsidiaries, and an India factory inaugurated in April 2025, which is intended to serve as a supply hub for South Asia and the Middle East. At MEDICA 2025, the company unveiled five new platform products — CBChroma™ (CBC blood analyzer), Chemichroma (clinical chemistry), ichroma 50 Plus (stool analysis), Urochroma (urinalysis), and Hemochroma 2 Plus (hemoglobin analyzer) — signaling a strategic transition from an immunodiagnostics specialist toward a full-spectrum POCT provider. Domestic Korean revenue has grown approximately fourfold from roughly KRW 3 billion in 2020 to approximately KRW 12 billion in 2025.
