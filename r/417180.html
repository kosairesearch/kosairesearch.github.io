Its core business centers on two genre-specialized webtoon platforms: Mootoon (male-oriented, wuxia/action genres) and QToon (female-oriented, romance/BL/fantasy genres). Based on prior disclosures, the content revenue mix was approximately 50% digital comics, 38% web novels, and 11% webtoons, complemented by advertising revenue and IP licensing through major platforms such as Kakao Entertainment and Naver for overseas distribution. This acquisition pivots FingerStory's profile from a pure-play webtoon platform toward a content-and-beauty conglomerate.
