Business

Guyoung Tech is a specialized automotive parts manufacturer established in 1989 in Gyeongbuk Yeongcheon, listed on KOSDAQ in 2002, and now headquartered at the Daegu National Industrial Complex in Dalseong. Its vertically integrated process—stamping through electrodeposition coating to assembly—and in-house progressive die design underpin cost and quality competitiveness.
