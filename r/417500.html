Business

JI-Tech was founded in 2014 and listed on KOSDAQ in November 2022 as a specialty chemical company focused on semiconductor and display materials. Its flagship product is semiconductor precursors for CVD/ALD deposition, spanning Si, Ti, Zr, and Hf variants, with the company estimated to hold Korea's largest Si-precursor production capacity. Globally, Entegris and Air Liquide dominate precursor supply, but Korea's memory manufacturers account for over 70% of global DRAM/NAND output, cementing JI-Tech's structurally significant domestic position.
