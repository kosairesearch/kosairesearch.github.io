Business

Korea FT (established 1984) is a specialist manufacturer of carbon canisters, plastic filler necks, intake systems, and interior/exterior trim parts. The carbon canister — which adsorbs hydrocarbon vapors from a fuel tank using activated carbon and re-combusts them when the engine runs — is a mandatory emission-control component. Korea FT completed domestic production in 1987 and has since maintained an exclusive supply position to all five domestic OEMs: Hyundai, Kia, GM Korea, Renault Korea, and KG Mobility. The company holds approximately 9% of the global canister market, ranking 4th worldwide, with a certified product lineup covering LEV-III (North America), KLEV3 (Korea), EURO-6d (Europe), and CHINA-6 (China) standards. Its plastic filler neck — domestically developed in 1993 — is over 45% lighter than steel equivalents, contributing directly to vehicle weight reduction and fuel efficiency, and is produced solely by Korea FT in Korea. The customer base is rapidly diversifying beyond Hyundai/Kia to include GM Global, Volkswagen, Renault, Nissan, Porsche, and Volvo. Korea FT operates eight overseas production bases across five countries — China (Beijing, since 2003), India, Poland, Slovakia, and the U.S. — in addition to its Anseong headquarters and domestic plants. The controlling shareholder is Italian auto-parts firm SIS S.R.L. (34.91%), and multiple private equity firms reportedly evaluated a stake acquisition in 2024, keeping an M&A overhang in play. New business initiatives include the world-first patented heated-type HEV canister, an EREV-dedicated canister nearing mass production, deep-learning ADAS software, and an in-vehicle virtual image monitor (VIM). Management has publicly stated a KRW 1 trillion revenue target for 2030.
