Business

Meere Company (formerly Meere Engineering, founded 1984) was renamed in 2004 and listed on KOSDAQ in 2005, specializing in display and semiconductor manufacturing equipment. The company's medium-to-long-term strategy centers on broadening its ultra-precision platform across display, semiconductor, and medical device segments to reduce dependence on display industry cycles.
