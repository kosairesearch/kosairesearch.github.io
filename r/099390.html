Domestic competitors include APM specialist Exem and OpenMaru, while global SaaS platforms such as Datadog, Dynatrace, and New Relic are expanding their Korean enterprise footprint. The company completed entry into the AWS Marketplace in 2024 and is pursuing Azure Marketplace listing to establish additional indirect global sales channels. A long track record of government deployments combined with CC and GS product certifications provides a meaningful procurement advantage in the public-sector tender process.
