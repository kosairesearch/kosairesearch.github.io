Key customers include HD Hyundai Infracore (estimated at 30–40% of consolidated revenue), HD Hyundai Construction Equipment, TEREX (US), HITACHI and KOBELCO (Japan), VOLVO (Sweden), and GENIE (US). The competitive landscape is oligopolistic, limited primarily to KYB of Japan and Hengli of China, with non-standardized design and a 5–10-year OEM qualification cycle acting as formidable barriers to entry.
