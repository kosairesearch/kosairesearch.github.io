The Concrete Products division produces ready-mix concrete, high-strength PHC piles, Hume pipes, and electric utility and telecom poles, operating two plants in Gwangju — one concrete products plant and one ready-mix plant. The Energy Solutions division manufactures EV chargers alongside residential-grade charging units and home-network solutions in line with the government's EV adoption policy. Primary customers are domestic construction companies, making revenues directly sensitive to local project award and construction activity cycles.
