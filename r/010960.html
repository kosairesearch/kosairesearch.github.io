Business

Samho Development (010960) is a KOSPI-listed construction company founded in 1976, with civil engineering as its core business: expressways, national roads, subways, ports, reclamation, and residential and industrial land development. Recent project activity includes GTX-B Section 4 in Seoul's Jungnang district (targeting 2028 completion), and road construction across Gyeonggi, Gyeongnam, and Seoul, reflecting deliberate geographic diversification. Regional competitors include Gyeryong Construction and Uwon Development, though Samho maintains construction capability leadership within the Sejong-Chungnam axis.
