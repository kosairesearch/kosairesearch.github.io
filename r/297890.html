Business

HB Solution, founded in 2001 and listed on KOSDAQ, is a dedicated manufacturer of display and semiconductor process equipment with overseas subsidiaries in Vietnam, Taiwan, and China. Its business is organized around four segments: display front-end, display back-end, inkjet applications, and semiconductor metrology. In the front-end display segment, the company produces ultra-precision analytical instruments—including thickness gauges and lighting inspection systems—using proprietary optical measurement, mechatronics, and nano-metrology technologies. Back-end display products center on automated coating, inspection, and bonding systems (BPL, FOD, UTG, ELB), with the OLED light-leakage prevention coater (ELB) serving as a key cash-generating product that has expanded from smartphones to tablets and laptops. The inkjet segment originated with OLED display patterning equipment and is now diversifying into secondary batteries, semiconductor thin-film protection, and biotechnology. The semiconductor pivot revolves around Nano-MEIS (Medium Energy Ion Scattering), a non-destructive thin-film analyzer for semiconductor oxide layers that previously had no domestic supplier. Samsung Display and Samsung Group affiliates accounted for more than 86% of 2024 revenue, though HB Solution has recently initiated trade with Chinese panel makers including BOE and CSOT to broaden its customer base. Parent company HB Technology holds a 29.52% stake, and both entities jointly invested as limited partners in a private equity fund holding HPSP shares, creating a structurally important non-operating income variable.
