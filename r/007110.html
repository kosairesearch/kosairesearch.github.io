Given the multi-faceted nature of the stone industry, the company maintains close ties with the logistics and trading sectors, and stands apart as the only domestic operator capable of handling all stages of the stone industry value chain. Primary customers are public and private construction project developers, creating meaningful sensitivity to construction-cycle fluctuations. The company has strengthened environmentally friendly operations through the adoption of wire-saw technology to significantly reduce noise and dust, and by maintaining a reforestation deposit for land rehabilitation after quarrying.
