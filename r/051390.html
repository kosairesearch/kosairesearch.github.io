The corporate finance division leverages the company's substantial equity base (₩84.4B at end-2025) to generate financial returns through the deployment of surplus capital, contributing materially to quarterly net profit. Customers are predominantly corporate clients served through operating lease structures designed to reduce their asset management burden. In competitive terms, dominant players such as Coway, LG Electronics Rental, and SK Magic are concentrated in B2C household appliance rental, while YW occupies a differentiated niche in B2B specialty equipment, IT devices, medical instruments, and corporate finance. This dual model is designed to capture both the secular shift from ownership toward usage-based consumption and corporate demand for asset-light balance sheets.
