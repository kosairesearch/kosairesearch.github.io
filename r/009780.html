The business is primarily B2B-oriented, supplying raw and processed ingredients to food manufacturers, food service operators, and beverage makers. Consolidated subsidiaries include MSC Myanmar and Shanghai Aimeixi Food Trading (China), providing offshore raw material procurement and local distribution infrastructure. FSSC 22000 certification and HACCP compliance across all products underpin its food safety positioning, complemented by ongoing high-quality new product development through advanced research facilities. Headquartered in Soju-dong, Yangsan, South Gyeongsang Province, MSC's key competitive assets are its specialized carrageenan and agar technology and its affiliate's agar supply capability.
