Business

Igis Value Plus REIT (KRX: 334890) is a KOSPI-listed public REIT managed by Igis Asset Management (IAM), the fund's dedicated AMC and Korea's ten-year consecutive #1 real estate asset manager with approximately KRW 67 trillion in AUM. Listed in July 2020 with Taepyeongno Building as its seed asset, the REIT employs a re-indirect structure, investing through real estate fund units rather than holding properties directly—a format that had historically excluded it from REIT ETF inclusion. The portfolio is divided into 'Core' assets (targeting at least 70% allocation) for stable dividend income and '+Alpha' assets (up to 30%) targeting capital appreciation; by September 2025, the Core allocation had risen to 89%, reflecting a stability-oriented rebalancing. Core assets include Taepyeongno Building and Gwanghwamun Twin Tree Tower (both CBD), Samsung Heavy Industries' Pangyo R&D Center (PBD), and a portfolio of 12 North American data centers tenanted by Microsoft and other global tech companies. The +Alpha sleeve includes Bundang Hostway IDC (responsible lease through June 2034), Icheon YM Logistics Center (100% leased to Samsung Electronics Logitek, expiring December 2026), and ISCO Chemical's Banpo headquarters. The Samsung HI Pangyo R&D Center was added in late 2024 via a sale-and-leaseback structure alongside institutional co-investors through a joint venture format. Since IPO, total portfolio assets have grown more than fivefold to approximately KRW 1.6 trillion as of September 2025, with semi-annual distributions paid on February and August reference dates.

The re-indirect structure means the fund holds real estate through a layer of private fund units, and individual fund maturities, co-investor exit timelines, and asset disposal schedules can constrain management flexibility across the portfolio. For assets where the REIT's equity stake is relatively small—such as the North American data center portfolio and the Samsung HI Pangyo R&D Center—decision-making authority is limited, reducing the ability to react nimbly to changing market conditions.
