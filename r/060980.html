Business

HL Mando operates 19 global production sites and is diversifying its customer base beyond Hyundai-Kia to GM, Ford, Chinese local OEMs, and European and Indian automakers. The automotive parts distribution business sustains competitive differentiation through an advanced logistics management system and benefits structurally from steady aftermarket demand growth.
