SKAI (SKAI Worldwide Co., Ltd.), founded in 2013 and listed on KOSDAQ in 2021 via a technology-special listing, rebranded from Bitnine in January 2025 and is Korea's only graph-database patent holder now positioned as an AI solution provider.
