The overseas revenue share has risen from approximately 31.9% in 2023 to around 38.9% as of Q3 2024, with the US and Japan accounting for the vast majority of exports. The SC100 launch monitor, introduced in 2014, achieved the No. 1 market share in portable launch monitors in the US; the upgraded SC4 PRO debuted in the US in 2024. All manufacturing is outsourced, allowing the company to concentrate resources on high-value R&D, planning, and marketing in an asset-light model. Primary global competitors include Bushnell, Garmin, Nikon, and GolfBuddy, while Voice Caddie holds a strong brand advantage in the domestic Korean market.
