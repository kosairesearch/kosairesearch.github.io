Business

Pharos IBio, founded in 2016 and listed on KOSDAQ in July 2023 under the technology-growth special-listing track, is a pure-play AI drug discovery company. Key sub-modules include ChemGen for novel compound generation, DeepRECOM for indication expansion, and a proteomics-based target-discovery module; the company also participates in NVIDIA's BioNeMo startup program to further enhance the platform. The principal pipeline comprises lasumotinib (PHI-101) for AML, PHI-501 for refractory solid tumors, PHI-601 as a next-generation menin inhibitor, and PHI-301 for intractable lung cancer, with PHI-701 and other early-stage assets rounding out the portfolio. A US subsidiary serves as the North American and FDA-approval beachhead, while an Australian subsidiary drives clinical trials and European market access. Chemiverse is reserved exclusively for in-house pipeline development rather than offered as a service, making clinical milestones and licensing deals the primary metrics for validating platform value. Domestic AI drug-discovery rivals include Syntekabio, while globally Insilico Medicine and Exscientia occupy overlapping territory.
