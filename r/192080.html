Business

DoubleU Games is a global game-content company developing and operating social-casino, iGaming and casual games centered on North America and Europe. Its key subsidiaries include DoubleDown Interactive (DDI) in the U.S., SuprNation in Canada/Sweden, and Paxie Games in Korea/Turkey, and in July 2025 it completed the acquisition of German social-casino firm WHOW Games. About 70% of consolidated revenue comes from DDI, with North America exceeding 90% of sales. Its core cash cows are long-running slot titles such as DoubleU Casino and DoubleDown Casino, now over a decade old, underpinning stable cash flows. Unlike social casino, iGaming involves real-money betting and withdrawals, and can only be operated by licensed firms in certain countries such as the U.K. and U.S. In casual games, Paxie Games now offers more than 55 titles and has surpassed 57 million cumulative global downloads. The company holds roughly a 5.9% share of the global social-casino market, ranking around fifth. Because social-casino services are effectively blocked in Korea on gambling-related grounds, essentially all of its revenue is generated overseas. A differentiator is its ownership of Nasdaq-listed DoubleDown Interactive as a subsidiary.
