Business

JMI (033050) was founded in Hwaseong, Gyeonggi Province in 1993 and listed on KOSDAQ in 1997, building its initial business on an Authorized Replicator (AR) agreement with Microsoft to supply PC software packages and record media. As PC digitalization eroded the record-media market, the company launched a living-goods OEM/ODM business—detergents, fabric softeners, and related household products—from 2020 onward, and officially reclassified its industry to 'chemical products manufacturing' in April 2024. Domestically, the living-goods OEM/ODM segment is now the core, with Aekyung Industries as the founding client (since 2020) and Henkel Korea added as a full-scale OEM partner from 2025, broadening customer diversification. The strategic priority is to shift from pure contract manufacturing (OEM) toward ODM—where JMI handles product planning and formulation—to capture higher margins over the medium term. The U.S. subsidiary has operated automotive interior-component manufacturing since 2021 and is currently expanding via a new plant in Georgia to meet growing customer demand. The U.S. entity also continues legacy record-media activities including software packaging, KIT assembly, and specialty labeling, which serve as a stable cash-flow contributor. The Vietnam subsidiary (JMI VIETNAM) produces electronic component trays and supports the global manufacturing network. Overall, the business remains predominantly an OEM/ODM contract manufacturer with concentrated exposure to a small number of key customers.
