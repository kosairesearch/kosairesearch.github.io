Business

Sillajen (215600) is a KOSDAQ-listed biopharmaceutical company founded in 2006, headquartered in Jung-gu, Seoul, focused on oncology drug development. Its pipeline spans three pillars. Second, Pexavec is a recombinant oncolytic virus with Phase 1/2 development in renal cell carcinoma, melanoma, and prostate cancer, with global licensing negotiations in progress. In March 2025, the company acquired IV drug specialist Wooseong Pharmaceutical for KRW 12.5 billion and completed an absorption merger in Q2 2025, transitioning from a pure R&D biotech to an R&D-led pharmaceutical company with a commercial division.
