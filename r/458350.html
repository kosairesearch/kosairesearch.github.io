ESteem was founded in 2004 as Korea's first dedicated model management agency, rebranded in 2017, and merged with ESteem Entertainment in 2024 to become a full-service fashion content company. With approximately 68 employees, ESteem operates as a compact specialist firm whose long-term growth thesis rests on a content-artist-brand flywheel ecosystem.
