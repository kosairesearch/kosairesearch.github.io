Business

Humedix was founded in 2003, rebranded in 2010, and listed on KOSDAQ in December 2014 as the aesthetics arm of Huons Group. Its core HA (hyaluronic acid) technology platform spans fillers, botulinum toxin, osteoarthritis drugs, ophthalmic products, cosmetics, and CMO/CDMO services supplied to domestic and global healthcare markets. The two main business divisions are Aesthetics and CMO/CDMO; key aesthetics products include CE- and NMPA-certified HA fillers Elravie and Revolain, botulinum toxin Lizztox, and medical device Derma Shine. Since 2025, the portfolio has expanded materially: ECM skin booster Elravie Ritu-O (exclusive domestic rights from L&C Bio), PLA filler Estefil (rights acquired December 2025), fat-dissolving injectable Ollifit (launched December 2025), and hospital cosmetic Ridle Booster (launched October 2025). As of Q3 2025, product sales represent approximately 80% of revenues, with a domestic/export split of 76%/24% (DailyInvest, January 2026).
