Business

PNC Tech (237750), established in 1999, is a power IT specialist that develops and manufactures digital power equipment for distribution grid applications. Its core product portfolio includes distribution automation terminal units (FRTUs), digital protection relays, SCADA systems (Pylon 1.0), advanced metering infrastructure (AMI), EV chargers, and residential kitchen fire suppression systems. Both the FRTU and digital protection relay have been designated as next-generation world-class products by the Korean government. Korea Electric Power Corporation (KEPCO) is the primary domestic customer, and the company has grown into South Korea's leading provider of distribution automation equipment for smart grids. Overseas markets span Turkey, Latin America, India, North America, Europe, and the Middle East, targeting both emerging and developed economies undergoing grid modernization. The company also operates a software solutions segment with proprietary SCADA software and railway fault indicator products. The consolidated scope includes affiliates related to the Gwangmyeong Electric investment; a small-scale merger was announced via DART in April 2026, and PNC Tech is restructuring its holding structure through an intermediary entity (H.K.
