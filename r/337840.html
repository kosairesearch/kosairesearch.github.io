Globally, Dexcom and Abbott dominate the CGM duopoly; domestically, i-SENS and IOflow have entered with enzyme-based products. UXN differentiates itself through foundational technology rather than competing on manufacturing scale or distribution, but the company remains in the pre-revenue clinical development stage.
