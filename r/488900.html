Business

The underlying cross-segment technology is special bonding—brazing and electron-beam welding—used to produce high-reliability structural parts requiring thermal resistance and hermeticity. Platform-based sharing of core technologies and facilities is the key competitive differentiator enabling efficient R&D and production scaling.
