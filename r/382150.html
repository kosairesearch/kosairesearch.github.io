Business

Its core technology, RAPTOR AI, analyzes transcriptomic (gene expression) data to match diseases with optimal drug candidates and expand indications for existing compounds — making OnCoCross the only domestic company specializing in Drug Development-stage indication expansion rather than early-stage compound identification. The company also develops ONCO-RAPTOR AI for oncology-specific analysis and ONCOfind AI for early cancer detection and cancer of unknown primary diagnosis. Domestic partners include JW Chong-wae Pharma, Donghwa Pharma, Daewoong Pharma, and Boryung; overseas collaborators include France's 4P Pharma and Switzerland's Alpha Mol Sciences. The in-house pipeline comprises OC514 for sarcopenia (Phase 1 completed in Australia), OJP3101 for acute myocardial infarction (Phase 2 in preparation), and OC212e for pancreatic cancer (investigator-initiated clinical trial ongoing). In 2025, OnCoCross signed an MOU with Seoul National University Hospital to leverage clinical data for AI drug discovery.
