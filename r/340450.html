Business

GC Genome is a clinical genomics analysis specialist established in 2013 as a subsidiary of GC녹십자홀딩스 (GC Group), supplying genomic services to over 900 hospitals and clinics, including 45 major tertiary general hospitals in Korea. Beyond MCED, GC Genome offers over 300 test types, continuously expanding into high-value precision diagnostics such as comprehensive cancer panels and rare disease panels. The G-MRD (minimal residual disease) technology addresses cancer recurrence monitoring and treatment efficacy assessment, enabling a full-cycle precision medicine platform from screening to post-treatment surveillance. Domestic competitors include iMBDX, GenesWell, Macrogen, and Cytogen; GC Genome is reported to hold a stronger domestic G-NIPT market position than global prenatal leader Natera.
