Manufacturing subsidiary ITM Semiconductor is expanding in e-cigarette components and the wearable device market. This diversified portfolio spanning financial information, payments, and infrastructure underpins cyclical resilience and positions NICE Holdings to orchestrate synergies across its subsidiaries.
