Business

NPK Co., Ltd. is a KOSDAQ-listed chemical company established in 1987 through a technology and capital joint venture with NIPPON PIGMENT, Japan's largest plastic raw material processor. Its core product portfolio comprises general-purpose plastic compounds (base resins such as PP, PE, and ABS blended with specialty additives and pigments), engineering plastic compounds (high-performance materials including PA and PBT grades), and color and functional masterbatches.
