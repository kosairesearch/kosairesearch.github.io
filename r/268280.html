Business

Miwon Specialty Chemical (Miwon SC) was established in 2017 via a spin-off from Miwon Holdings and trades on the KOSPI. Its core business is producing acrylic monomers and oligomers—the essential building blocks of energy-curable (UV/EB) resins that replace conventional solvent-based systems with faster-curing, eco-friendly processes delivering superior gloss, hardness, and scratch resistance. End-use applications include coatings, printing inks, adhesives, and electronic materials, with growing direct and indirect use in TFT-LCD panels, semiconductor processes, automotive coatings, and 3D printing. Key domestic customers include LG Chem and Kolon, while international clients include Switzerland's RAHN AG and Japan's TOYO Chemical. Sales channels combine direct-to-end-user sales and distribution through intermediaries. The company operates two production plants and one sales office, and a new 60.5bn-KRW energy-curable resin facility at Wanju Techno Valley is under construction with a target completion date of December 2026. Concurrent R&D in water-dispersible resins, bio-based materials, and LED-curing-compatible monomers is building the next generation of growth-oriented product lines.
