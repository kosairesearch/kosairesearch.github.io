Business

Core products are Analog ICs and Logic ICs—classified as system semiconductors—supplied across automotive, home appliance, motor controller, display power management, and circuit breaker applications. A central pillar of its long-term strategy is the expansion of automotive IC revenue, which has grown from just 5% of total sales in 2013 to 28% as of 2023.
