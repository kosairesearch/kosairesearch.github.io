Business

Curiosis was founded in 2015 by researchers from Seoul National University's Department of Mechanical Engineering and is a bio materials, parts, and equipment (bio-SOBUJAN) company specializing in lab automation solutions for drug discovery and cell therapy research. Its core product portfolio includes Celloger (an automated live-cell imaging system capable of real-time in-incubator observation with ±0.1°C temperature stability), Cellpuri (an automated cell separation/purification system), MSP (a digital pathology system), and the Colony Picker System completed in May 2025. Based on 2024 revenue mix, automated live-cell imaging accounts for ~66.7% and other revenue (consumables, services) ~33.2%, while the lab automation segment is still nascent but expected to grow rapidly through ODM expansion. Revenue channels consist of two pillars: own-brand direct sales and ODM (original design manufacturing) through global life-science partners. The customer base spans domestic and overseas drug developers, academic and research institutions, CDMO companies, and government procurement agencies. The proprietary 'Curios-System' platform, with 100% in-house core component integration, underpins the product lineup, supported by a ~6-month new-product development cycle that continuously expands the IP portfolio. Competitively, Curiosis targets niche markets against global giants such as Hamilton, Yokogawa, and Revvity, leveraging its proprietary precision actuation and imaging technology alongside competitive pricing.
