The company runs offline and online channels in parallel through dedicated import/export and online-marketing subsidiaries, and also holds equity stakes in China-based health-related businesses, combining operating and investment holding characteristics. CXI plans to scout and contract additional K-indie brands via a newly formed TF team throughout the year, accelerating a dual-track strategy that spans both health food and cosmetics distribution. In health foods, competitors include domestic Chinese brands and premium imported products; in K-beauty wholesale, the company competes with numerous domestic and international distribution agents active in China. CXI is classified under the 'Finance' sector on KOSDAQ, reflecting its Cayman Islands holding structure, and stands as one of the first-generation Chinese companies listed in Korea with over 16 years of listing history.
