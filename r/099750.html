Business

EzCareTech was established in 2001 as a spin-off from the IT department of Seoul National University Hospital and listed on KOSDAQ in 2019. Its core business spans three pillars: on-premise HIS 'BESTCare 2.0' development, sales, and installation for large and mid-sized hospitals; system operation and maintenance (SM) services; and cloud-based HIS 'EDGE&NEXT' for small-to-mid-sized hospitals and clinics. With approximately 26–30% share in the tertiary-hospital HIS segment, it holds the top domestic position and is the only vendor with double-digit implementation references at Korea's leading tertiary hospitals. It was the first domestic vendor to obtain Ministry of Health EMR system certification, with key clients including Seoul National University Hospital, Bundang Seoul National University Hospital, veterans' hospitals, and the National Cancer Center. Domestic competitors include Ubcare and Bit Computer (focused on smaller clinics) and Univers Global and Infinite Healthcare (competing for public HIS contracts), but no rival matches its simultaneous multi-hospital deployment capability at tertiary scale. Internationally, the company operates through a Saudi joint venture (Saudi Korean Health Informatics Company), EZCARETECH USA in California, and EzCaretech Japan, accumulating roughly KRW 61 billion in Middle East contract awards since its 2014 Saudi debut. The business is diversifying into digital healthcare through AI voice-recognition EMR partnerships, telemedicine solutions (ezOntheCall), and healthcare big-data analytics (K-MIMIC). Revenue is roughly balanced between project delivery and stable recurring SM contracts, and the company has declared a long-term target of capturing 50% of the domestic HIS market by 2030.
