Business

Production is anchored at a facility in Vietnam, contributing to cost competitiveness, while overseas sales are managed through dedicated subsidiaries in the United States, Slovakia, and China. Beyond seat heaters, iWIN has expanded its portfolio to include ventilated seats, radiant warmers, and heated steering wheels to improve product mix quality. In May 2026, the company completed the disposal of land (22,840 m²) and buildings (21,617 m²) in Busan Gijang to Kaim Co. for KRW 22.8 bn, accelerating non-core asset rationalization and financial improvement.
