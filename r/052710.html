Business

The global AI server MLCC market is dominated by Murata and Samsung Electro-Mechanics (~90% combined share), with Amotech positioned as a challenger targeting niche segments including EV and AI optical-network applications, leveraging ceramic materials expertise and price competitiveness to broaden its global customer base.
