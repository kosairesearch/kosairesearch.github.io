Business

Its four operating segments are: ① high-school (approx. 61% of 2024 revenue), ② early childhood through middle school (approx. 23%), ③ university transfer (approx. 8%), and ④ employment/other (approx. 8%) (EduPlus, April 2025; based on DART 2024 annual report).
