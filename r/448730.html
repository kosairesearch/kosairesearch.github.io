Samsung FN REIT is a sponsor REIT backed by Samsung Financial Networks (Samsung Life and Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance), established in July 2022, licensed in October 2022, and listed on KOSPI in April 2023 as a prime Seoul office specialist. Asset management is handled by Samsung SRA Asset Management. Under Korea's Real Estate Investment Company Act, at least 70% of total assets must be invested in real estate. At IPO, the REIT held two assets: FN Tower Daechi (GBD) and FN Tower Sunhwa (CBD). In September 2024, a ₩64.2bn rights offering (expanding shares from 77.3mn to 91.05mn) funded acquisition of FN Tower Pangyo (Samsung Fire's Pangyo HQ, BBD). Dividends are paid quarterly in January, April, July, and October, with an average yield of approximately 5.5% since listing.
