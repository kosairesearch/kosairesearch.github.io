Key clients include T-Mobile and Verizon in North America, SoftBank, J:COM, and NTT East in Japan, and Orange and Vodafone in Europe. Competitive pressures come from domestic peers such as Humax and global majors including Huawei, TP-Link, and NETGEAR, but the company's broad global customer network and high export concentration serve as meaningful differentiators.
