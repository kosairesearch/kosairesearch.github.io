Business

Joy Works N Co (formerly Oheim & Company), listed on KOSDAQ, specializes in the design, manufacture, and distribution of furniture and consumer goods with a primary focus on online channels. Its core brand portfolio includes 'Lady Furniture,' 'For the Home,' and 'Aidenew.' In 2024, the company incorporated 3D-AI as a subsidiary and acquired part of Joy Works' operations, renaming itself Joy Works N Co.
