Carelabs was established in 2012 and listed on KOSDAQ in 2018 as a mobile healthcare and beauty-care platform company; since Wonik Holdings became its controlling shareholder in January 2023, it has pursued operational restructuring under CEO Lee Min-kyung. Channel synergies with Wonik affiliate CMS Lab (beauty brand CellFusionC) and a new Senior Care ('Care Town') business targeting the super-aging demographic represent the stated long-term growth drivers.
