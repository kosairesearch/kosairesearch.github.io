Business

E8 (E8ight) is a deep-tech company specializing in simulation software and digital twin platforms, established in 2012 and listed on KOSDAQ in February 2024 under the technology-growth special listing scheme. Target end markets span smart cities (B2G), manufacturing, secondary batteries and semiconductor processes, energy and plant operations, building energy management, and defense and aerospace. Globally, the competitive landscape is dominated by large incumbents including Siemens, Dassault Systèmes, PTC, and ANSYS; domestically, E8 differentiates through the uniqueness of its particle simulation technology, though a significant resource and sales capability gap relative to global peers persists.
