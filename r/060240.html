Business

Starco Link operates a diversified structure spanning three distinct segments: marine cabin equipment and interior supply, modular housing construction, and mobile game publishing. The 2024 absorption merger with Starco Co. elevated the marine cabin division into the primary revenue driver. In the marine cabin segment, the company supplies onboard living and common-area fittings while providing Total Turnkey Engineering services; an OEM manufacturing agreement with a Chinese cabin component producer is pursued for cost reduction. The modular housing segment delivers Infill- and Stacking-method modular structures targeting residential and commercial demand. The mobile gaming segment operates through subsidiary Taigon Mobile, generating IP royalty income through developer collaborations. Diversification offers some risk dispersion, but the limited scale of each unit constrains overall operating efficiency. Growing global vessel orders have improved Korean shipyard order intake, driving downstream demand for cabin suppliers and placing Starco Link's core business in a favorable demand environment. Listed on KOSDAQ in 2002, the company has undergone multiple restructurings to reach its current composite business model.
