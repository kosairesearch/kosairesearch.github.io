Founded in 1992 as a microfiber textile specialist and listed on KOSDAQ in 2003, Welcron has evolved into a diversified group spanning textiles, construction, plant engineering, and energy. Welcron Hantec's energy affiliates (iWell, iWell Energy, Ariwel Energy) operate Jeju biomass power generation and wastewater sludge recycling ventures, but recent profitability weakness at these entities has burdened consolidated group finances. Looking ahead, Welcron is pursuing eco-friendly material development — including recycled-PET yarn and biodegradable fiber — alongside artificial blood vessel localization as future growth pillars, aiming to pivot its textile technology expertise toward higher-value applications.
