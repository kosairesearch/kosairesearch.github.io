Business

SY Donga (formerly DongA Hwasung), founded in 1974, is a specialty manufacturer of rubber components for automotive, home appliance, and industrial applications, listed on KOSDAQ since 2001. In December 2025, Samyoung Partners—a wholly-owned subsidiary of KOSDAQ-listed Samyoung Mtek—acquired a 42.18% stake at KRW 20,000 per share, installing new majority ownership; the company subsequently rebranded as SY Donga in April 2026. Business is divided between two core segments: automotive and home appliances, with Researcha (May 2026 estimate) attributing approximately 50.8% of revenue to automotive and 49.2% to home appliances. The automotive segment supplies rubber and plastic components—including air intake hoses, intercooler pipes, and battery-pack gaskets—to Hyundai Motor and Kia, with intercooler pipe volumes rising alongside the Hyundai Motor Group's expanding hybrid vehicle lineup. The home appliance segment delivers drum washing machine rubber components to LG Electronics, Samsung Electronics, and other global appliance makers. Overseas subsidiaries in India, China, and Russia underpin a global delivery network; key raw materials include synthetic rubber, fillers, plasticizers, and compounding agents. Through 72.5%-owned subsidiary SY Donga Fuelcell, the company has commercialized 5 kW (high-temperature) and 10 kW (low-temperature) building-use hydrogen fuel cell systems. New growth initiatives in precision rubber parts for humanoid robot actuators and defense components are being pursued in parallel.
