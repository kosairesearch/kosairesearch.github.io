Business

Skonec Entertainment (Skonec), founded in 2002, is a specialist in XR (extended reality) content and operates XR gaming, XR education and training, duty-free shopping brokerage, and real estate investment on a consolidated basis. In 2024, Skonec officially launched 'Strike Rush' — co-developed with Meta Platforms — on the Meta Quest official store, and also released the psychological horror title 'Who's at the Door?' via Smilegate's Stove platform, broadening the game lineup. In 2025, an attempt to diversify revenues through a 100% acquisition of Jeju BK (an LPG distributor) was ultimately cancelled. A KRW 10B convertible bond issuance and board reshuffle followed, with the controlling shareholder expected to transition from founder Hwang Dae-sil through Glometa (formerly Unicorp) to FBH Global Investment Fund. As of September 2025, the company employs 75 people and holds 23 patents.
