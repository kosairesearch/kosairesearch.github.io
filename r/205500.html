Business

The games segment leverages the 10-year-old 'Blade' IP across titles including 'Three Kingdoms Blade', 'ANVIL', 'Smashing the Battle', and 'Metallic Child'; 2025 saw record revenue driven by the global rollout of 'Rohan 2 Global'. In the AI domain, the AI-agent battle platform 'MoltyRoyale' — built in just 60 hours — was launched in February 2026 and has since accumulated over 22 million agents.
