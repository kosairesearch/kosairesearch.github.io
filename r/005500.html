Business

Key OTC products include the analgesic/anti-inflammatory Geborin and appetite stimulant Trestan, while the ETC lineup features antithrombotic Flearis, antibiotic/antifungal Taurolin, antibiotic Klaricid, brain-function improver Neutyrin, and cholesterol drug Newstatin. The co-promoted pain patch Norspan Patch has established itself as a roughly KRW 10bn annual revenue product, while flu vaccines Fluad and Flucelvax generated KRW 3.6bn in their debut quarter following a 3Q 2025 launch. The WishHealthy health functional food brand (HaRuEngine line) and medical-device expansion represent the company's strategy to diversify beyond reimbursed pharmaceuticals. R&D is centered at the Magok Research Center (opened 2021), running 16 active pipeline projects across immune-inflammation, oncology, and CNS areas—plus AI drug discovery and ADC teams—while managing over KRW 10bn in government grants. Under second-generation family management in place since 2025, newly established oncology and pulmonary arterial hypertension divisions underpin the strategic pivot toward high-value specialty therapy areas.
