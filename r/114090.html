Business

GKL (Grand Korea Leisure) is a KOSPI-listed operator of the foreigners-only casino brand 'Seven Luck.' It currently operates three foreigners-only casinos: Seoul Gangnam-COEX, Seoul Dragon City (Yongsan), and Busan Lotte. The bulk of revenue comes from casino gaming, split between table and machine games. Its customer base consists of Chinese and Japanese VIPs and mass-market players, with recent diversification into Hong Kong, Taiwan, the US, and Thailand.
