Business

Komelon was founded in 1963 as a metal and textile tape measure manufacturer and listed on KOSDAQ in 2001. Operations center on two main segments: the tape measure (measurement tools) segment and the cold-rolled steel (rolling mill) segment. The rolling mill segment is operated from the Incheon plant. Backed by a sales network spanning 80 countries, the company is fundamentally export-driven; the United States is its single largest market, accounting for 62.9% of total revenue in 2025. Komelon ranks 3rd–5th globally in the tape measure market and holds No. 3 brand awareness in the US. The group operates seven subsidiaries including a US entity, with the Vietnam plant serving as the critical low-cost production hub.
