Core product lines include Daelim-seon fishcakes and surimi (approximately 25% domestic market share), Sajo tuna cans and ham, and Haepyo cooking oil, marketed under the Sajo, Haepyo, Daelim-seon, and Oyang brands. The fishing segment leverages the Sajo Group's deep-sea fleet for vertically integrated supply of Alaska pollock and tuna. Exports of frozen gimbap and other HMR products targeting Generation MZ are a newer growth initiative. As the group's food sector hub, Sajo Daerim serves as the strategic centerpiece of Chairman Joo Jin-hong's vision to build an integrated national food enterprise.
