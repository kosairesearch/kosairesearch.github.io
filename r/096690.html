Business

Aroot is a KOSDAQ-listed SME headquartered in Osan, Gyeonggi-do, whose core business is the manufacture of industrial mini printers—POS, label, mobile, and RFID—under its proprietary 'SEWOO' brand. In the domestic POS printer market, Aroot forms an oligopoly with EPSON and Bixolon, with the three firms collectively commanding 80–90% market share; globally, it competes against Japan's EPSON, Star, and Citizen, and the US's TPG and Ithaca, within a concentrated market where the top four players control over 65%. The consolidated group comprises five segments: the printer business (including subsidiary Sewootech), an electronics distribution arm (official Xiaomi Korea partner), an uninterruptible power supply (UPS) business through subsidiary Jionix, a semiconductor equipment refurbishment unit (NSRC), and an eco-friendly waste recycling operation (Aroot Eco). Aroot Eco operates a ~20,000 m² resource recycling plant in Seosan, South Chungcheong Province, with annual processing capacity of 75,000 tonnes of waste plastic. Since early 2026, the company has been extending its footprint further into blockchain-based digital finance via the 'FORTA' platform—a stablecoin issuance, payment, and settlement infrastructure—which has already completed integration with Visa's global card payment network. The company is thus in a structural transition from a single-category printer manufacturer to a multi-segment business portfolio.
