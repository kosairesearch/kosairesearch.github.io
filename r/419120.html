Business

Sandoll was established in 1984 as Korea's first font foundry and listed on KOSDAQ in 2022; its two core pillars are the cloud-based SaaS subscription service 'Sandoll Cloud' and custom corporate brand-font development. Launched in 2014, Sandoll Cloud has grown to 2 million subscribers, supports 14 languages, and hosts 97 corporate partners. The June 2024 completion of the Yoon Design Group acquisition—creator of the iconic 'Yoon Gothic'—pushed the combined domestic font-platform market share to approximately 90%. Blue-chip corporate font clients include Samsung, Alibaba, Naver, LINE, Coupang, Baemin, and Riot Games. Subsidiary Sandoll MetaLab operates the stock-image platform Vivi Tree and provides AI image-generation and ML-based font-search services. In Q1 2026, the company launched 'Font Playground,' a generative AI font creation service developed through collaborations with LG CNS, NC AI, and Inswav, and signed a dedicated brand-font MOU with BGF Retail (CU). A structural lock-in effect—where canceling a subscription breaks fonts in existing creative files—drives a 95% customer retention rate and underpins a stable, recurring-revenue foundation.
