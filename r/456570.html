Business

IMGT was founded in April 2010 in Seongnam, Gyeonggi-do, as a focused ultrasound (FUS)-based drug delivery platform company, co-led by CEO Lee Hak-jong (concurrent professor at Bundang Seoul National University Hospital) and CEO/CTO Son Geon-ho. Its core technology, ChemoSono Therapy®, employs focused ultrasound to temporarily create micropores in tumor cell membranes, dramatically enhancing intratumoral drug penetration without the need for exogenous microbubble agents—regarded as a world-first approach.
