Business

Jincostech, founded in 2010 in Ansan, Gyeonggi Province, is a mask-pack-focused ODM/OEM cosmetics manufacturer listed on the Konex market. Its core product line is hydrogel mask packs and patches (79.95% of revenue), which together with sheet mask packs (10.27%) bring the total mask-pack segment to 88.21% of sales. Products cover facial, eye, lip, hand, foot, and neck applications, complemented by basic cosmetics such as lotion, cream, cleansing foam, and essence. The business model is almost entirely ODM/OEM-based at 99.95% of revenue. ISO-GMP and CGMP certifications underpin manufacturing quality, and the company also manages proprietary brands—AnyPack, BEAUBeaute, and HealSkin—for direct overseas market access. CEO Kim Im-jun, a textile-engineering graduate who pioneered mask-pack development in the late 1990s, holds a 43.50% controlling stake. The competitive landscape features large generalist ODMs (Cosmax, Korean Kolmar) alongside mask-pack specialists (Janik, C&C International); Jincostech differentiates through hydrogel expertise and flexibility in serving small-to-mid-sized indie brands.

A pivotal structural shift in 2025 was the surge in total equity from roughly KRW 2.4 bn to KRW 18.8 bn, driven primarily by RCPS-to-common-stock conversions, which cut the debt-to-equity ratio from 1,305% to 177%.
