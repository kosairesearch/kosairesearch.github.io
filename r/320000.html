Business

HanWool Semiconductor was founded in 1999 as Wintech Co. and listed on KOSDAQ in 2020; it rebranded to its current name in 2024 and repositioned itself as an AI-driven semiconductor inspection specialist. Its core business is the design and sale of precision inspection equipment powered by machine vision and image-processing technology, organized around three pillars: MLCC visual inspection machines, display bonder-line inspection systems, and secondary-battery copper-film inspection equipment. In the MLCC visual inspection segment the company holds a leading position among domestic small-and-mid-sized customers and also supplies equipment to major clients including Samsung Electro-Mechanics and LG Display. In April 2026 it commenced development of a hybrid pressure-mark and electrode integrated inspection machine targeting the display sector. Key domestic peers include Intekplus, Gigavis, Nextein, and V1 Tec.
