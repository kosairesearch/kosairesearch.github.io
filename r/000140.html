Business

The holding company's own revenues derive primarily from brand and trademark license fees and dividends from subsidiaries, supplemented by unified management of intellectual property including the 'Hite,' 'Chamisul,' and 'Jinro' trademarks. The 'other' segment includes still water (Seoksu), ethyl alcohol production, and golf course operations, providing some portfolio diversification.
