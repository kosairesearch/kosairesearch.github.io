Core manufacturing takes place at WITS VINA in Vietnam, with ongoing automation and yield improvements supporting cost competitiveness. Samsung SMAPP-certified wireless charging set products are distributed through global retail channels, and the company is pursuing a medium-to-long-term roadmap into next-generation beamforming wireless charging and EV wireless charging.
